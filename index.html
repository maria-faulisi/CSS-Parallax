Vera

Smothers

She was born Vera Smothers in Metropolis, Illinois. Metropolis, the home of Superman, where boys leapt tall buildings in a single bound, and girls learned to cook biscuits n' gravy. They spoke softly but were made of steel.

McDaniel

As a McDaniel, Vera moved to Chicago from Tennessee following the death of her husband. He was 35 when he died. She was a single mother of 8.

Mother

All of her kids called her "Mother". Never "Ma" or "Mom" or "Mommy" always "Mother". She worked as a waitress and somehow supported them all. She once saved her tips for a month to buy Gary a pair of Converse Chuck Taylors.

BeBaw

Of all the names she ever went by, BeBaw was the most widely used. No matter how hard times may have been, she always made sure to look her best. She broke her arm once because she refused to take her garbage out in anything other than heels. She was one of a kind.
